How to Play

Welcome to Flip-dle!

  • Guess the target 5-letter word within 4 tries.
  • You must build your words using only the provided 8-letter bank.
  • The Twist: Some letters can be physically flipped 180° to create new letters (e.g., flipping d creates a p).
  • Click the ↻ corner icon on a tile to flip it before adding it to your guess!
